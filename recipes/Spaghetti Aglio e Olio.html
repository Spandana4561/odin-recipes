Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic (thinly sliced)
  • Olive oil
  • Red chili flakes
  • Parsley (chopped)
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic until golden.
  3. Add red chili flakes and stir.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti and a bit of pasta water to the pan.
  5. Toss well to coat with oil and garlic.
  6. Mix in chopped parsley and Parmesan if desired.
  7. Serve hot.
