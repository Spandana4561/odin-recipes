Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Mix until just combined (don't overmix).
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Lightly grease if needed.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on top.
  6. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or fruits.
